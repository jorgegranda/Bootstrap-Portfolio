About Me

For more than a thousand years, My Tibetan Family of Coding and Web Design Experts have played a key role in the cultural life of the Internet World. Our efforts have permeated virtually every facet of Coding and Web Design. They were commissioned by internet sleuth's and or by pious individuals like Tim Berners-Lee for use within the practice of Mahayana Coding.

My doctrine paves the way for the belief in a coding-buddha-nature that is inherently present in all beings and which can be realized through the proper procedures and practices.
